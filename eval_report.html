Draw infection cards equal to the current infection rate (shown on the track: 2, 2, 2, 3, 3, 4, 4). Place one disease cube of the matching color on each city drawn. If a city already has 3 disease cubes of that color when it would receive another, an outbreak occurs instead. ### Outbreaks When a city already has 3 disease cubes of one color and would receive another cube: - Do not place a cube in that city. - Move the outbreak marker one space forward on the track. - Place one cube of that color in each city connected to the outbreak city. If any connected city also already has 3 cubes, a chain reaction outbreak occurs — that city also triggers an outbreak (but cities that already triggered outbreaks this turn do not trigger a second time). If the outbreak marker reaches the last space on the track (8 outbreaks), the players immediately lose.

p.7 This passage outlines the unique abilities of each role in the board game, detailing how they interact with the game's mechanics, such as removing disease cubes, discovering cures, and managing city cards, which are critical for answering rules questions about gameplay strategies. ## Role Abilities ### Medic The Medic removes **all** disease cubes of one color when treating a disease, using only 1 action. When a disease is cured, the Medic automatically removes all cubes of that color in any city they enter or are already in. ### Scientist The Scientist needs only **4** cards of the same color (instead of 5) to discover a cure. ### Researcher The Researcher may give any city card to another player in the same city during the Share Knowledge action — the card does not need to match the current city. ### Operations Expert The Operations Expert may build a research station in their current city without discarding the matching city card. Once per turn, they may move from a research station to any city by discarding any city card. ### Dispatcher The Dispatcher may move any other player's pawn to a city containing another pawn as an action. The Dispatcher may also spend their actions to move another player's pawn as if it were their own. ### Quarantine Specialist The Quarantine Specialist prevents disease cube placement and outbreaks in the city they are currently in **and all cities directly connected to it**. ### Contingency Planner The Contingency Planner may take any event card from the infection discard pile and store it on their role card. There is no hand limit for the stored card, and it can be played at any time. When used, the card is removed from the game.

If you draw an epidemic card, follow the epidemic procedure immediately. ### Epidemic Procedure 1. **Increase** — Move the infection rate marker one space to the right. 2. **Infect** — Draw the bottom card of the infection deck and place 3 disease cubes on that city. 3. **Intensify** — Shuffle the infection discard pile and place it on top of the infection deck.
